About Me

I am a full stack web developer based in New York City. For as long as I can remember, I always loved building things, whether it was with other people or by myself. My hard work and passion for technology landed me a contract IT position at Texas Instruments, one of the biggest semiconductor companies in the world. After my contract ended, I enrolled in and graduated from the New York Code + Design Academy, a full-time 12-week course in full stack web development designing and building web applications.

In addition, I hold a B.A. in Graphic Design from CSU Northridge, where I held the position of Team Leader of S.A.G.A. (Student Advertising Graphics Association).

As a freelancer, I offer the following services to my clients: full stack web development, mobile development, graphic design, and UX/UI design.
